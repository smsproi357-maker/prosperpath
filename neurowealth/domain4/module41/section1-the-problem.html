Section I · Why Liquidity Exists

Every Trade Needs Two Sides

Every trade requires both a buyer and a seller.

If someone wants to buy a large amount, there must be enough

If there are not enough sellers at the current price, price must move to find them.

Order Example

Buyer wants 100 BTC
Available now 20 BTC Deficit: 80 BTC
↑ Price must move higher to find more sellers.

Markets move in search of orders.

Order Clusters Create

When many orders gather at the same level, that area becomes

These clusters form from different types of orders, all placed at similar price levels for different reasons.

Stop Losses

Traders place stop losses just beyond obvious levels to limit their risk.

Breakout Orders

Traders place buy or sell orders expecting price to break beyond key levels.

Pending Limit Orders

Traders wait at specific price levels expecting a reaction or continuation.
